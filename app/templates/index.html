Our Vision & Mission

Discover what drives us and how we work to develop the Python ecosystem in Togo and create opportunities for developers.

Who We Are

Python Togo is a dynamic community dedicated to fostering Python development in Togo. We provide opportunities for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and skills building for developers at all levels.

Founded by passionate Python enthusiasts, our community has grown to include developers from across Togo who share a common interest in Python programming and its applications.

We believe in the power of community and collaborative learning to create a strong Python ecosystem that can contribute to technological advancement in Togo.

Our Foundation

The pillars that guide our community's growth and activities

V

Vision

Our vision is to create a dynamic and innovative Python ecosystem in Togo that is recognized regionally and internationally. We aspire to make Togo a hub of excellence in Python development in West Africa, where Python-based technologies and solutions significantly contribute to the country's socio-economic development.

We envision a community where every Togolese Python developer is equipped with the skills and resources needed to excel in the global tech industry and address local challenges through innovative solutions.

M

Mission

Our mission is to promote the adoption and use of the Python programming language in Togo through education, collaboration, and innovation. We are committed to:

  • Educating and training the next generation of Python developers
  • Facilitating knowledge sharing and collaboration among members
  • Creating professional opportunities for developers
  • Developing innovative technology solutions to solve local problems
  • Establishing strategic partnerships with other organizations and businesses

Our Goals

Strategic objectives that drive our community forward

1

Skills Development

Regularly organize training sessions, workshops, and hackathons to improve the technical skills of Python developers in Togo.

2

Community Growth

Achieve a vibrant community of more than 1000 active members by 2026, with representation in all regions of Togo.

3

Strategic Partnerships

Establish strong partnerships with educational institutions, businesses, and other tech communities to create a robust Python ecosystem.

4

Community Projects

Initiate and successfully implement open source projects that address local challenges and actively involve community members.

5

International Visibility

Represent Togo at international Python events and establish connections with other Python communities worldwide.

6

Inclusion and Diversity

Ensure fair and diverse representation within the community, with a particular focus on increasing female participation.
